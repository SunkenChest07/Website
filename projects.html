Things I've been working on

Cybersecurity Plan

I have an Obsidian(Notetaking App) vault that I have been working on that has detailed lists of software and hardware that you can use as well as habits you can utilise in your everyday life to protect yourself mainly digitally but in the end may also protect you physically.
This of course is a work in progress and might take me a little over a couple of moths to finish "everything", I put quotes on that becuase I highly doubt that there's a definite end to complete security and privacy for everyone. But I feel having a crackshot plan for myself and then sharing that plan with other people around me is the best way to reach that.

Oh the things you can do with Raspberry Pi

While I didn't have any specific plans for just one Pi the wonder that is the internet has hundreds of maker projects which include Pi type devices in them, from Media Centers to NAS's, to a personal DNS and Web servers. These little things can do a lot, which gives these little things a lot larger a use case than just a "small computer".
