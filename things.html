My Computer

Or should I say laptop considering I don't own a desktop. But anyway,
Its a light ASUS laptop with 4GB of ram and a 1.10GHz dual core processor. So basically no "EXTREME" gaming on this thing. But I should talk about how when I first got this laptop it was running Windows 11 and along with that, I was still in school so that meant constant Outlook updates and sending Microsoft Word documents back and forth. Meaning that I would have to wait just a little bit until Linux could be my daily driver. So Immediately the night after graduating with an ATAR of 46 I Downlaoded the Linux Mint Debian Edition (LMDE) .ISO file, flashed that sucker onto the nearest flash drive I had and by the next day your boy was feeling at home in his new operating system.
